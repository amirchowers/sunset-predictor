# Sunset Predictor Brand Brief

## What we are
A premium sunset intelligence brand.

## What we publish
Daily Instagram visuals predicting sunset quality.

## Audience
Urban people who care about beautiful evenings, city atmosphere, photography, rooftops, beach walks, and aesthetic utility.

## Positioning
Not a weather channel.
Not a meme account.
Not generic AI lifestyle content.
A refined visual intelligence product.

## Desired feel
- atmospheric
- premium
- trustworthy
- useful
- sharp
- restrained

## Voice
- short
- confident
- not cute
- not overexcited
- not poetic for the sake of it

## Core content blocks
- location
- sunset time
- score
- verdict
- 2 to 3 drivers behind the score
- optional short action line only if strong
